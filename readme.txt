== Description ==

*Details: With the help of the Private Ad plugin you can show ads to your blogs you just have to select the category name and add the banner. it is very easy for you Note: this plugin is only for private ads you can promote your other website or other business by Private Ad plugin Google ad code will not work. It's totally free. Feel free to share your feed at teamlead@dreamreflectionmedia.com.
	  

*Admin section: A Easy and Beautiful admin section will help you to track all the Ads that you have made with the Private Ad plugin. You can edit & delete any of the Ads that you want. there are a total of three pages in the Private Ad plugin the first one is 
1. Advertisement: where you can add your ads.
2. My category: where you can see the all categories that you have and who created the categories and how many posts are there in the categories by just clicking the category name. 
3. My ads: here you can see All your ads and here you can edit or delete your ads.  

*Support: Private Ad Plugin is a community-driven project that would not be the same without your feedback. If you find any problem or feature request for this plugin, please feel free to contact us at teamlead@dreamreflectionmedia.com or Pankaj.b@dreamreflectionmedia.com! 

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to Create Ads? =

First, install the Private Ad Plugin and after activating the plugin you will see a go-to button click to go on the plugin tab in the dashboard. Here you will see a form where you have to fill in all the related details about the Ad like company name link URL ETC. 

= Where can I reach the documentation? =

https://dreamreflectionmedia.com/

=How do I choose the categories name ?=

Just simply go to the Advertisement tab where you will see all categories in the dropdown inside the from.

=what should I do if I choose the wrong banner for the ad?=

In the case you choose the wrong banner and submit please go to the My ads tab and here you can Edit or delete your ads

=What should I do when I experience any problem?=

Contact us at teamlead@dreamreflectionmedia.com or Pankaj.b@dreamreflectionmedia.com
